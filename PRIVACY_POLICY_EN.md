## 6. User Rights

### 6.1 Right to Access
- Users have the right to request a copy of their personal data
- You can download your data at any time from the app

### 6.2 Right to Correction
- You can edit your personal data directly in the app
- You can update any inaccurate information

### 6.3 Right to Deletion
- You can delete your account and all associated data
- Data will be deleted from all systems (local and cloud)
- Data cannot be recovered after final deletion

### 6.4 Right to Data Portability
- You can obtain a copy of your data in a portable format
- You can transfer your data to another service

### 6.5 Right to Object
- You can object to the processing of your data at any time
- We will stop processing your data upon request (unless legally required)

### 6.6 Right to Informed Consent
- We only share data with your explicit consent
- You can withdraw your consent at any time

## 7. Data Retention

### 7.1 Retention Period

**Active Account Data:**
- Data is retained as long as the account is active
- Data is secure only for the active member

**After Account Deletion:**
- Data is permanently deleted from all systems
- The app maintains backups for a maximum of 30 days
- After that, all backups are deleted

**Logs and Backups:**
- Access logs are deleted after 90 days
- Backups are deleted after 180 days
